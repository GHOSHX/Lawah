How to use Lawah

Home Page

  • Click to create a new article.
  • Click to upload a backup a file of a old article if you've saved one.
  • Click on an article to open it.
  • Click to rename the article.
  • Click to delete the article.

Article Editor

  • Click to edit the article.
  • Click to save a backup file.
  • Click to upload a backup a file of a old article if you've saved one.
  • Click to open settings

Settings

  • Click to show it.

Rows

  • Click to add an infobox row.
  • Click to add a category row.
  • Click to add a text area row.
  • Click to add a table row.
  • Click / to move a row up/down.
  • Click to delete a row.
