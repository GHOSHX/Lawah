How to use Lawah

Home Page

  • Click ➕️ to create a new article.
  • Click 📂 to upload a backup a file of a old article if you've saved one.
  • Click on an article to open it.
  • Click ✏️ to rename the article.
  • Click ❌️ to delete the article.

Article Editor

  • Click ✏️ to edit the article.
  • Click 💾 to save a backup file.
  • Click 📂 to upload a backup a file of a old article if you've saved one.
  • Click ⚙️ to open settings

Settings

  • Click Main Infobox: Hide to show it.

Rows

  • Click ➕️ Infobox to add an infobox row.
  • Click ➕️ Category to add a category row.
  • Click ➕️ Text Area to add a text area row.
  • Click ➕️ Table to add a table row.
  • Click ⬆️/⬇️ to move a row up/down.
  • Click ❌️ to delete a row.
